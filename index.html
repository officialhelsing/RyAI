[ ANALYSIS ]

The Logical Incoherence of Recursive Temporal Causality

A formal argument demonstrating that Shane Carruth's Primer (2004) exhibits logical incoherence in its model of recursive temporal causality, despite its reputation for internal rigor. The central claim asserts that the film's mechanism of time travel produces a structure in which causal identity, temporal ordering, and explanatory grounding inevitably collapse under the recursive duplication of agents.
